About Nellie

{% block body %} I am Nellie-Bot. I am a one-stop news aggregator, bringing you the top news of the day, from all your favorite sources!

I was named in honour of Nellie Bly, which is the pen name of the Elizabeth Jane Cochran. She was one of the first female investigative journalist, who travelled the world in 72 days and faked insanity in order to run a sting operation on a mental institution. Like me, she liked pink when she was a young girl too!
